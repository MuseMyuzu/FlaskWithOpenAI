How To Use

Speak

Pressing "speak" starts recording, and pressing "send" stops recording and sends the voice to the server.

Language

You can select your language from the selection bar in the lower left corner. Select "Ja" to speak Japanese or "En" to speak English.

Volume

The volume can be adjusted with the volume bar. The left side is mute, and the volume gets louder as you go to the right.

Full Screen

On a PC, you can switch to full screen from the icon in the upper right corner. Press again to return.

Caution

  • The voice data is sent to a server I subscribe to, but I don't use it for anything other than this application.
