How To Use

Speaking

Pressing record_voice_over starts recording, and pressing send stops recording and sends your voice to the server.

Morse Code

  • "・" is dot, and "ー" is dash.
  • Characters that cannot be converted to Morse Code are enclosed in [].

Language

The selection bar in the lower left corner allows you to choose the language you speak. Select "Ja" to speak Japanese or "En" to speak English.

Volume

The volume can be adjusted with volume_up. The left side is mute, and the volume gets louder as you move to the right.

Full Screen

On a PC, you can switch to full screen from fullscreen in the upper right corner. Press again to return.

Caution

  • Your voice data will be sent to my contracted server, but will not be used for anything other than this application. The audio will be deleted as soon as the transcription is complete.
